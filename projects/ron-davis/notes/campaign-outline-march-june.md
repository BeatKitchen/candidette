# Friends of Ron Davis — March–June 2026 Campaign Priorities
*Candidette Campaigns | Elizabeth Rosenberg dba Candidette Campaigns*

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (March 16–31)
*Goal: Get the infrastructure in place before activation begins.*

**Campaign Operations**
- Create campaign timeline and calculate vote goals
- Begin managing candidate calendar
- Set up staff email coverage
- Create preliminary field plan: identify priority precincts

**Volunteer Recruitment & Management**
- Create interim volunteer tracking document
- Create volunteer sign-up system (Mobilize, Linktree, etc.)
- Create special event calendar — identify public opportunities April–June

**Endorsement Support**
- Create endorsement tracking spreadsheet
- Begin identifying endorsement opportunities with candidate

---

## Phase 2: Building (April)
*Goal: Recruit the team, develop materials, begin outreach.*

**Campaign Operations**
- Leverage Votebuilder/CRM for voter contact and script tracking
- Continue calendar management and email coverage

**Volunteer Recruitment & Management**
- Begin recruiting general canvass volunteers
- Begin recruiting "special function" volunteers (social media, turf cutting, events, website)
- Create simple canvass script for early voter outreach

**Endorsement Support**
- Begin endorsement outreach with candidate
- Schedule endorsement meetings as needed

---

## Phase 3: Activation (May)
*Goal: Get volunteers in the field. Candidate visible in the community.*

**Campaign Operations**
- Continue calendar management, email coverage, and CRM tracking

**Volunteer Recruitment & Management**
- Create canvass training materials and checklists for super-volunteers
- Support "train the trainers" — prep super-volunteers to lead canvass launches
- Increase turf cutting with greater support from special function volunteers

**Endorsement Support**
- Continue endorsement outreach and tracking

---

## Phase 4: Handoff & Wrap (June 1–8)
*Goal: Leave the campaign set up to continue without a gap.*

- Transition volunteer tracking to Votebuilder or CRM
- Brief super-volunteers on ongoing responsibilities
- Deliver final materials, checklists, and calendar
- Wrap endorsement tracker and note outstanding outreach
