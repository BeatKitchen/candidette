# What Claude Can Do with Your Google Account

Nathan set up a secure connection so Claude can read and update your Google Sheets, Docs, Calendar, and Drive — but only the specific files you choose to share.

---

## How It Works

Claude uses a special email address to access your Google files. It works exactly like sharing a document with a coworker:

- **If you share it, Claude can see it**
- **If you don't share it, Claude can't see it**

That's it. You control what Claude has access to.

---

## How to Share Something New with Claude

Let's say you create a new spreadsheet for a client and want Claude to be able to read and update it.

1. Open the Sheet (or Doc, or Drive folder)
2. Click the **Share** button (top right)
3. Paste this email address:

   > *(Nathan will fill this in after setup)*

4. Set the permission:
   - **Editor** = Claude can read AND make changes
   - **Viewer** = Claude can only read, not change anything
5. Uncheck "Notify people"
6. Click **Share**

That's all you need to do. Next time you ask Claude about that file, it'll be able to help.

---

## How to Remove Claude's Access

If you ever want to stop Claude from seeing a file:

1. Open the file
2. Click **Share**
3. Find the Claude email address in the list
4. Click the dropdown next to it → **Remove**

Done. Claude can no longer see that file.

---

## What Claude Can Do

Once something is shared, you can ask Claude things like:

- "What's on my calendar this week?"
- "Create a project tracker for the new client"
- "Add the filing deadline to my calendar"
- "Update the budget spreadsheet — we got a $5K donation"
- "Pull my notes from today's meeting doc"
- "What files are in the Sam Wang folder?"

Claude will do the work and confirm what it did.

---

## What Claude Can NOT Do

- Send emails on your behalf (not connected)
- Access files you haven't shared with it
- See your browsing history or other apps
- Make changes without telling you what it did

---

## If Something Breaks

Just tell Nathan. The fix is almost always one of:
- The file wasn't shared with Claude's email
- The connection needs a quick refresh

Nothing you can break from your side.
